About the Founder

Vidur Nayyar is a strategically focused and results-driven leader with over a decade of proven experience in leading AI and digital transformation efforts across multiple industries, including insurance, banking, and healthcare. He has been instrumental in spearheading AI-driven innovations and operational enhancements, collaborating closely with CXOs to deliver high-impact solutions. He is on the board of multiple organizations pushing the boundaries of AI. He holds a Master's in Computer Engineering from Rutgers University and continues to contribute to the industry through mentorship and thought leadership.
